Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between raw and compounded hing? expand_more

Raw hing is pure resin, which is extremely pungent and hard. Compounded hing (like our Bandhani variant) is blended with edible flours to make it easier to use in daily cooking while maintaining a balanced aroma.

How should I store AD Spices Hing? expand_more

Always store hing in an airtight container, preferably in a cool, dark place. Avoid using wet spoons to maintain its longevity and aroma potency.

Is your product gluten-free? expand_more

Our compounded variants may contain edible wheat or rice flour. Please check the label for specific ingredients or contact our support team for specialized dietary requirements.
